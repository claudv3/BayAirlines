Why Bay Airlines?

Bay Airlines is a brand new airline based in the San Francisco Bay Area that starts its operation in January 2026! By choosing to fly with us, you will expect discounted rates for the first few months of operation and be able to fly to many destinations! Come check us out!

About Us

Bay Airlines is founded by Marie and Bob Garcia, a mother and father wanting to seek big changes within the airline industry. They wanted to create an airline to fly anywhere, specifically to and from the Bay Area, while promoting the Bay Area to the world. This airline’s home base is at San Francisco International.
